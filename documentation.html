About LD Music?

This project came up to life, because of the need of a music bot to our main community Legendary Dust.

  1. A lot of people asked for a public version of LD Private Music.
  2. We've thought that a music bot without any premium limitations would be very cool, so here is LD Music Public. What we offer?:
      • Fast - Get response to commands instantly.
      • Qualitative - Listen to high quality music.
      • Completely free - There are no premium subscriptions you have to worry about. We give our services for free, but there are some command that are limited to a short cooldown, because of performace reasons.

Commands

here is a list of all commands that LD Music provides. Commands that are marked with ⭐ are being limited to a cooldown

Keep in mind that the examples are using the default l! prefix. It may differ on the prefix you've set.
Arguments that are between [] brackets are required. Those with <> are optional.
Category Command Description Usage
Music play play's music from Youtube l!play [search query/Youtube link]
Music skip skips to the next song in the queue, or if provided the argument, skips to the number in queue (queue should be 2 songs minimum length). l!skip <number in queue>
Music join if the bot is not connected to a voice channel in current server, this command will make the bot to join and stay on your voice channel. l!join
Music disconnect if the bot is connected to a voice channel in current server, this command will make the bot to join and stay on your voice channel. l!disconnect
Music loopqueue toggle the loop of the queue. l!loopqueue
Music loop toggle the loop of the currently playing song. l!loop
Music stop stop's the song, and destroy's the player (removing all songs in queue if any). l!stop
Music queue display's the first 30 songs of the queue, if the queue is larger than 30 songs you can see the second page using l!queue 2 and so on. l!queue <page number>
Music volume ⭐ set the volume of the playback. Min-max volume: 0-200 l!volume [number]
Music nowplaying display's the currently playing song l!nowplaying
Music clearq clear's all the song's in the queue, if there is a song playing, it will not be affected. l!nowplaying
Music clearq clear's all the song's in the queue, if there is a song playing, it will not be affected. l!nowplaying
Music lyrics find the lyrics of a song. l!lyrics [song name]
Music filter set's a filter for the song, currently we have only 2 filter available (8D and lower pitch, also known as slowed song effect). l!filter [filter]
Music shuffle shuffle's the whole queue and set's a new random position. (Useful for custom playlist's COMING SOON) l!shuffle
Music remove remove a song from the queue, if you accidentaly added the wrong song. l!remove [number in queue]
Settings setprefix set-up your own prefix, you can also mention bot before using a command. l!setprefix [new prefix]
Settings player toggle the player information. l!player [option]
Settings language BETA choose a language for your server. l!player [available language]
General links set-up your own prefix, you can also mention bot before using a command. l!setprefix [new prefix]
General about toggle the player information. l!player [option]
General updates BETA choose a language for your server. l!player [available language]
General aliasesBETA see a list of available aliases, also you can check them here. l!player [available language]
